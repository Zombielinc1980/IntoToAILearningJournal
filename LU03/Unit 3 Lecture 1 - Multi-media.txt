Unit 3 Lecture 1 - Multi-media

Multi-media: Any format of media (photos, videos, audio)

AI Generated Multi-media: Media generated by AI, video games generated by AI, media altered by AI

Text-to-speech: using a piece of text that gets read out by an automated voice

Voice Cloning: Using an audio sample to create a piece of audio that the person didn't say

Text-to-video: using a written prompt to generate a video

Image-to-video: using an image prompt to generate a video

Garbage in, garbage out

AI art tends to come out yellowish, it believes the yellowing will make it look better.
